Red Pasta Recipe

Description

A great non traditional dish consisting of pasta.

The recipe isn't for the faint of heart cause it's ultra hot.

Ingredients

  • Pasta 500g
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Red Hot Chilli Peppers chopped (no pun intended) as many as you can handle (I put 6)
  • 2 tspns of tomato paste
  • 3 fresh tomatoes chopped in small pieces
  • Pot
  • Big frying pan
  • 3 tbspns of olive oil
  • 1 big onion chopped in micro pieces
  • 1 small piece of garlic

Steps

Cooking

  1. Place water in the pot. Put the pot on the stove on high heat.
  2. Wait for it to boil. Throw pasta in the pot. Follow the instructions on the packaging to check required time for al dente.
  3. Place pan on the stove on high heat. Throw oil in. Throw onion and garlic in.
  4. Simmer for 2 mins. Throw peppers in. Wait for 2 mins.
  5. Throw tomato paste in and tomatoes salt and pepper. Add 100ml of water.
  6. After a few mins it will start to boil. Let it boil for 10 mins.

Serving

  1. Combine the pasta with the sauce.
