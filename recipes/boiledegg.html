Boiled Egg Recipe

Description

A great traditional dish consisting of one boiled egg.

The recipe isn't that easy to execute but i believe you can make it.

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Pot

Steps

Cooking

  1. Place the egg in the pot.
  2. Fill the pot with water until it covers the egg.
  3. If the egg floats, replace the egg since the previous one is rotten.
  4. Fire up the stove and place the pot on it.
  5. The setting for the stove is high heat.
  6. Wait until the water starts to boil.
  7. Set a timer for 4 mins if you want the egg soft boiled, 5 mins for medium boiled and 6 mins for hard boiled.
  8. Turn off the timer.
  9. Remove the egg from the stove and turn off the stove.
  10. Add cold water in the pot and wait for 2 mins for the egg to cool off, repeat if necessary.
  11. Peel the egg.

Serving

  1. Place the egg on a plate.
  2. Cut the egg in half.
  3. Sprinkle salt and pepper.
  4. Add a drop of olive oil on each half.

Eat
